A Local SpikeBall League For Everyone

Ultra competitive? Pretty chill and just looking for a day in the sun? Or anywhere in between?

Our local SpikeBall leagues are super fun and Covid Safe!

Bring your group of friends, your team mate, or even just yourself and play the game with people just like yourself!

  • Our balls and commonly touched surfaces are sanatized prior to each new competition for your safety. Masks are required between strangers and encouraged between groups that sign up together.

  • You can choose between different levels of competition:

    • Play for money
    • Play competitively but with no prize
    • And relaxed play

  • You can sign up with a group, with a partner, or by yourself.
    We make partners and matches based on experience, skill, as well as what category you choose (competitive/relaxed).

  • We have a concession where you can buy pre-packaged sandwiches and drinks.
